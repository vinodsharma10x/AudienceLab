## CUSTOMER JOURNEY MAPPING - SAMPLE 2
### Dog Training Program for Reactivity Reduction (Spain)

### PHASE 1 · DISCOVERY

**Description:**
The owner begins to recognize their dog has problematic behaviors affecting their quality of life and the animal's. Typical triggers include reactivity episodes on the street (excessive barking, pulling, aggression toward other dogs or people), embarrassing public incidents, neighbor complaints, or inability to enjoy relaxed walks. Discovery process usually lasts 2-4 weeks, especially after a particularly stressful incident or when realizing they avoid certain places or situations due to dog's behavior.

**Emotions:**
- Frustration at not being able to control their dog (intensity 8/10)
- Shame in public situations when dog misbehaves (7/10)
- Anxiety before going for walks, anticipating problems (6/10)
- Guilt for not knowing how to help their pet (7/10)
- Desperation when home methods don't work (6/10)
- Growing curiosity about professional solutions (5/10)

**Customer Needs:**
- (Very high urgency) Immediate solutions to control street reactivity
- (High urgency) Safe and effective methods that don't damage relationship with dog
- (High urgency) Clear professional guidance on what to do and not do
- (Medium urgency) Understanding causes of problematic behavior
- (Medium urgency) Information about different training approaches

**Customer Knowledge:**
- Know basic home methods they've tried unsuccessfully (treats, scolding, choke collar)
- Know training professionals exist but don't know differences between methods
- Have seen YouTube videos or read articles, but information is contradictory
- Understand the problem is serious but don't know if it has a real solution

**Customer Feelings:**
- Daily stress anticipating walks (8/10)
- Deep love for their dog but exhaustion from situation (9/10)
- Hope that a definitive solution exists (6/10)
- Concern for dog's wellbeing and whether it suffers with these behaviors (7/10)
- Distrust of "miraculous" promises after previous failures (5/10)

**Customer Actions:**
- Search Google for terms like "reactive dog", "how to walk off-leash", "dog training Spain", "aggressive dog with other dogs"
- Consult Facebook groups like "Dog Owners Spain", "Positive Dog Training"
- Watch César Millán videos, canine educators on YouTube, look for Instagram testimonials
- Ask on forums like Perros.com or consult with veterinarians
- Frequency: 3-5 weekly searches for 2-4 weeks

**Customer Thoughts:**
- "Why is my dog like this? Am I the problem?"
- "It can't be normal that every walk is a battle"
- "Does a real solution exist or will it always be like this?"
- "Is it too late for my dog? He's 6 years old, can it still change?"
- "I don't want to use aggressive methods, but this can't continue"

**Customer Problems:**
- Lose 2-3 daily hours on stressful walks that should be relaxing
- Avoid parks, social encounters, family visits due to dog's behavior
- Deteriorated relationship with neighbors due to barking or incidents
- Restriction of family activities (can't take him traveling, to terraces)
- Economic expense on products that don't work (collars, special leashes, toys)

**Solutions You Can Offer:**
- Specialized reactivity training program with proven and respectful methods
- Free initial evaluation to identify specific behavior causes
- Personalized plan according to breed, age, and dog's reactivity level
- Guarantee of visible results in first sessions to generate immediate confidence

**Touchpoints:**
- Facebook and Instagram ads targeting dog owners in Spain (optimal timing: weekends when planning family activities)
- Short educational videos on TikTok showing real before/after transformations
- Articles in specialized blogs and collaborations with veterinarians and shelters
- Presence in local Facebook groups and specialized behavior forums

**Language and Terminology:**
- Keywords: "reactive dog", "pain-free training", "walk off-leash", "obedient dog", "reduce reactivity"
- Search terms: "my dog barks at other dogs", "how to train aggressive dog", "dog trainer near me"
- Hashtags: #ReactiveDog #DogTraining #ObedientDog #OffLeashWalk

**Key Metrics:**
- Average initial research time (2-4 weeks)
- Number of weekly stressful incidents (>3 per owner)
- Monthly searches related to "reactive dog" in Spain (>8,000)
- CTR on ads targeting owners with behavior problems

### PHASE 2 · CONSIDERATION

**Description:**
The owner evaluates different training options, comparing methods, prices, locations, and testimonials. Particularly focuses on trainer philosophy (positive vs traditional), guaranteed results, schedule flexibility, and geographic proximity. This process usually lasts 1-3 weeks, during which they may consult multiple professionals and read extensively about different approaches.

**Emotions:**
- Renewed hope finding specialized professionals (7/10)
- Confusion faced with variety of contradictory methods and philosophies (6/10)
- Frustration at significant price differences without understanding why (5/10)
- Cautious optimism seeing success cases similar to their situation (6/10)
- Anxiety about making the right decision and not worsening situation (7/10)
- Impatience to start the process as soon as possible (8/10)

**Customer Needs:**
- (Deal-breaker) Respectful methods that don't cause additional stress to dog
- (Must-have) Verifiable testimonials of similar successful cases
- (Must-have) Schedule flexibility compatible with work and family
- (Very valuable) Initial evaluation to understand specific plan for their case
- (Desirable) Flexible payment options and satisfaction guarantee

**Customer Knowledge:**
- Distinguish between traditional training (dominance) vs positive (reinforcement)
- Understand reactivity has multiple causes (fear, lack of socialization, genetics)
- Know importance of consistency and owner commitment
- Have researched different tools (clicker, treats, long leashes)

**Customer Feelings:**
- Relief discovering reactivity is treatable and common (8/10)
- Concern about time and energy commitment required (6/10)
- Enthusiasm imagining relaxed walks and freedom for dog (9/10)
- Nervousness about whether they'll be able to apply techniques correctly (5/10)

**Customer Actions:**
- Request free initial consultations with 2-3 different trainers
- Read detailed testimonials and look for real case videos on websites
- Check prices, available schedules, and specific methodologies
- Ask in specialized groups about experiences with specific trainers
- Frequency: 5-8 contacts/consultations during 1-3 weeks

**Customer Thoughts:**
- "Does this trainer really understand my specific type of dog?"
- "Are the testimonials real or just marketing?"
- "Will I be able to maintain the consistency training requires?"
- "Is it worth paying more for a specific method?"
- "How much realistic time will we need to see changes?"

**Customer Problems:**
- Information saturation contradictory about training methods
- Difficulty verifying real experience and trainer results
- Time pressure to make decision (current situation unsustainable)
- Concern about non-transparent additional costs (materials, extra sessions)

**Solutions You Can Offer:**
- Free 45-minute initial consultation with complete case evaluation
- Real case testimonial videos with 6-month follow-up
- Clear methodology explained step by step with scientific validation
- Personalized training plan with realistic timeline and specific guarantees

**Touchpoints:**
- Landing page with detailed case studies and transparent methodology
- Free weekly webinars on "Understanding Canine Reactivity"
- WhatsApp channel for quick consultations during decision process
- Collaborations with local veterinarians for cross-references

**Language and Terminology:**
- "Free consultation", "scientific method", "guaranteed results", "respectful training", "verified real cases"
- "Personalized evaluation", "realistic timeline", "continuous follow-up", "gradual transformation"

**Key Metrics:**
- Number of initial consultations requested per week (>15)
- Consultation to complete program conversion rate (>40%)
- Average decision time after initial consultation (3-7 days)
- Engagement level in webinars and educational content

### PHASE 3 · DECISION

**Description:**
The owner decides after successful initial consultation, where they see a clear and realistic plan for their specific dog. Final triggers are usually personal connection with trainer, confidence in proposed method, accessible payment plan, or urgency to resolve an unsustainable situation. Decision is typically made within 24-48 hours after last decisive contact.

**Emotions:**
- Deep relief at finding the right solution (9/10)
- Excitement to begin dog's transformation (8/10)
- Renewed determination to commit to the process (7/10)
- Gratitude toward professional for understanding their specific situation (8/10)
- Positive nervousness about coming changes (5/10)

**Customer Needs:**
- Clear training plan with specific weekly objectives
- Direct access to trainer for doubts during process
- Necessary materials and tools included or clearly specified
- Flexibility to adapt schedules according to family availability
- Visible results in first 2-3 sessions to maintain motivation

**Customer Knowledge:**
- Fully understand methodology and their role in process
- Know realistic timeline and expected weekly milestones
- Understand that whole family consistency is crucial for success
- Know what to expect in first sessions and how to measure progress

**Customer Feelings:**
- Genuine hope that life with their dog will significantly improve (9/10)
- Firm commitment to follow instructions to the letter (8/10)
- Relief at having professional plan vs continuing to improvise (8/10)
- Controlled impatience to start immediately (7/10)

**Customer Actions:**
- Sign contract and make payment (transfer, card, financing)
- Schedule first sessions according to mutual availability
- Prepare home environment according to trainer indications
- Inform whole family about new rules and required consistency
- Acquire specific recommended materials (long leash, special treats, clicker)

**Customer Thoughts:**
- "Finally we have a real and professional plan"
- "I'm prepared to do whatever is necessary"
- "When will we be able to see the first changes?"
- "I wish we had done this before, how much suffering we would have saved ourselves"
- "I'm going to document progress to see evolution"

**Customer Problems:**
- Anxiety about not correctly applying taught techniques
- Family coordination to maintain rule consistency
- Managing expectations about results timeline
- Possible temporary setbacks during learning process

**Solutions You Can Offer:**
- Structured 8-week program with clear objectives per session
- Exclusive WhatsApp group for students with 48h support
- Reinforcement videos of each technique taught for home practice
- Materials kit included and results guarantee or partial refund

**Touchpoints:**
- Welcome email with detailed timeline and first session preparation
- Personal call 24h before first session to resolve doubts
- App or platform for progress tracking and direct communication
- Private Facebook group to share experiences with other students

**Language and Terminology:**
- "Your transformation starts now", "personalized plan activated", "first session confirmed", "everything included"
- "Guaranteed follow-up", "continuous support", "step-by-step results"

**Key Metrics:**
- Consultation to hiring conversion rate (>50%)
- Activation time after hiring (<48 hours)
- Post-first session satisfaction (>90%)
- Full program adherence without dropouts (>85%)

### PHASE 4 · RETENTION

**Description:**
The client has successfully completed the basic program, sees tangible results in their dog, and seeks to maintain progress, perfect advanced techniques, and access the responsible owner community. Retention is based on continued results, post-program support, group activities, and access to additional specialized training. Many become active ambassadors of the method.

**Emotions:**
- Deep pride in transformation achieved with their dog (9/10)
- Satisfaction at being able to enjoy walks and family activities (9/10)
- Lasting gratitude toward trainer and method (8/10)
- Renewed confidence in their capacity as responsible owner (8/10)
- Sense of belonging to successful owner community (7/10)

**Customer Needs:**
- Periodic follow-up to maintain achievements and prevent setbacks
- Access to advanced training (agility, search, complex tricks)
- Active community to share experiences and receive advice
- Discounts on additional services and priority access to novelties
- Opportunities to help other owners with similar problems

**Customer Knowledge:**
- Completely master basic reactivity management techniques
- Can identify and prevent potentially problematic situations
- Understand their dog's body language and anticipate reactions
- Know advanced training and environmental enrichment techniques

**Customer Feelings:**
- Daily joy enjoying relaxed walks and activities with their dog (9/10)
- Pride receiving positive comments about their dog's behavior (8/10)
- Satisfaction helping other owners with their experiences (7/10)
- Loyalty toward method and professional who changed their life (9/10)

**Customer Actions:**
- Participate in quarterly or semi-annual reinforcement sessions
- Attend group workshops and special activities (canine hiking, agility)
- Share testimonials and actively refer new clients
- Participate in community events and owner meetups
- Sign up for advanced courses and specializations

**Customer Thoughts:**
- "I can't believe the difference, it's like having a completely new dog"
- "I want to keep learning and improving our relationship"
- "I love helping others going through what I went through"
- "This method really works, I'll always recommend it"
- "What else can I learn to keep enjoying with my dog?"

**Customer Problems:**
- Risk of relaxing consistency once basic objectives are achieved
- Need for new challenges to keep dog stimulated
- Possible new behaviors due to age or circumstance changes
- Desire to help others but without adequate pedagogical tools

**Solutions You Can Offer:**
- Maintenance program with quarterly sessions at reduced price
- Graduate club with monthly activities and exclusive discounts
- Ambassador program that allows helping new clients
- Specialized advanced courses (canine sports, assisted therapy)

**Touchpoints:**
- Monthly newsletter with advanced tips and event announcements
- Private WhatsApp/Telegram group for graduates
- Quarterly in-person events (hiking, friendly competitions)
- Online platform with exclusive content and consultation forum

**Language and Terminology:**
- "Graduate club", "premium community", "advanced techniques", "method ambassador"
- "Maintaining excellence", "new challenges", "expert owner"

**Key Metrics:**
- 12-month post-program retention (>70%)
- Graduate club activity participation (>40%)
- Referral rate per satisfied client (>2 references per year)
- Advanced program enrollment (>30% of graduates)
- Online community and event engagement level
